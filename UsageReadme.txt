## Usage

1. **User Registration**:
   - Users can register as either a **User(Job Seeker)** or a **Recruiter**.

2. **User**:
   - After logging in, Job Seekers are directed to the job listing page where they can:
     - View all available jobs.
     - Apply for jobs.
     - View all jobs they have applied for.

3. **Recruiter**:
   - After logging in, Recruiters are redirected to the recruiter page where they can:
     - Create new job listings.
     - Delete job listings.
     - View candidates who have applied for their job listings.
     - Update job listings.
     - Change the status of job listings.
